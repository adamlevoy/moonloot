About Moonloot

An Ethereum inspired brand, giving back to the Etherverse

Who we are

Monoloot is a passion project founded by Ethereans seeking to participate in and contribute to the Ethereum ecosystem. We are scratching our own itch for original Ethereum inspired merchandise, while building a channel that gives back to the Etherverse.


How we give back

We donate to Ethereum related Gitcoin grants voted on by our customers. Proceeds from the sale of our products go to our grant treasury, an Ethereum wallet. We curate a list of Gitcoin grants doing meaningful things in the Etherverse. Our customers vote on which grants we support.


Why support us

We promise to provide quality products with the peace of mind knowing that your purchase supports the Ethereum ecosystem. By supporting Moonloot, you're supporting Ethereum, and the future of a decentralized economy.
